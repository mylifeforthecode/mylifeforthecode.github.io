Hello! My name is Shawn Rakowski and welcome to my blog! I am a father, husband, programmer, blogger, and podcaster. I have an affinity for software development and the people in it, and would like to help as many people as I can to find happiness in this space.

I earned a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Philosophy from Bemidji State University in 2009. Since then I have been working as a software engineer and technical recruiter.

Being on the job since I graduated, I have come to the conclusion that my distaste for processes and rules means I am probably not going to be happy with most of the jobs in the corporate world.

I am currently pursuing failure in hopes of one day succeeding at something and escaping life in the corporate world forever. In 2015 I created a podcast to help encourage software developers who have hit a low point to rise up and find something that brings back their enjoyment of programming. The people I interviewed had great stories to tell and are inspiring to me. I hope they inspire you too! I may bring this back in the future, but I'm focusing on other things at the moment.

Thanks for visiting my blog, and feel free to contact me with any questions or comments. My email address is shawn@mylifeforthecode.com.

Until the glorious day that I am my own boss the views here are my own and do not necessarily reflect the views of my employer.
