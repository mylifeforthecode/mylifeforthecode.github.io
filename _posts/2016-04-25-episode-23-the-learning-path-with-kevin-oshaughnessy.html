In this episode I interview Kevin O'Shaughnessy. Kevin is a senior Web developer based in the UK. Kevin runs a blog at www.zombiecodekill.com and is also a regular guest blogger at Simple Programmer and Outlier Developer. He believes in continuous learning and improvement, and is doing a 12 learning paths challenge on Pluralsight in 2016. Kevin is also active on Twitter @zombiecodekill.
