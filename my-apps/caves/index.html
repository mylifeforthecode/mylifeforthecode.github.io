Caves is a bottom up scrolling game inspired by a game I used to play on my graphing calculator back in high school. The goal of the game is to make it down the cave as far as you can without being pushed to the top and out of the cave. Avoid hitting the sides of the cave and the obstacles marked by the X and you'll make it far!.

This one was for fun and is free, enjoy!
